• Start by uploading a class roster file (export from my.hanover, or Canvas csv).
  • You may also manually add students if you know their 6 digit ids.
  • If any of the generated names are not right, you can double-click and edit them.
  • You can click the "x" button at the top right of a photo to remove that student. Don't worry, the X should go away when you print.
  • You can rearrange the student order by simple drag and drop.
